Technical Analysis Webinar Series

Role: Speaker and Instructor

Project Description:
I had the privilege of leading a comprehensive three-part webinar series for PINA Trade users. This series was dedicated to unraveling the fundamentals of Technical Analysis. We covered an array of crucial topics, including the intricacies of price formation, the nuances of reading candlesticks and market trends, pinpointing valuable market areas, recognizing pivotal chart patterns, and crafting detailed trading plans.

My Role:
As the lead speaker and instructor, my role extended beyond just presenting information. I was instrumental in curating the educational content, ensuring that it was not only informative but also engaging. Delivering this content live through Zoom sessions, I focused on making each webinar interactive, comprehensive, and accessible to traders of all levels.

Outcome:
The response to the webinar series was overwhelmingly positive, drawing in over 1,000 participants. Its success went beyond numbers, as it played a crucial role in educating and empowering a new generation of traders in the Indonesian Stock Market. It was an opportunity to share insights, foster learning, and contribute to the growing community of informed traders.

Skills Utilized:

  • Deep expertise in Technical Analysis, translating complex concepts into digestible content.
  • Strong proficiency in creating and delivering educational content, ensuring it resonates with a diverse audience.
