Mean Reversion Automated Trading Strategy

Role: Quantitative Developer

Project Description:
As a Quantitative Developer at Barid Capital Management, I embarked on a challenging project to develop a mean reversion automated trading strategy. This strategy leveraged the unique mean reverting properties of the currency markets, involving extensive analysis, conceptualization, rigorous testing, and meticulous optimization.

My Role:
My responsibility was to transform theoretical concepts into a practical, fully-functional trading strategy. This involved in-depth analysis of market patterns, creating algorithms to exploit these patterns, and integrating advanced financial theories with quantitative methods for a robust strategy.

Outcome:
The strategy yielded a significant +15% annual return while maintaining a modest 5% average annual drawdown, demonstrating an effective balance between risk and reward and highlighting its reliability in various market conditions.

*Performance over 8 years data or 600 trades

Skills Utilized:

  • Applied advanced financial and quantitative analysis for strategy development.
  • Used MQL5 for coding and implementing the trading algorithms.
  • Employed Excel for detailed strategy optimization and testing.
  • Managed a VPS for efficient and uninterrupted strategy deployment.
